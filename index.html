Ray Zirui Xu

I am a Ph.D. student in Aerospace Engineering at University of Michigan, advised by Vasileios Tzoumas. My research interests lie in algorithmic robotics, in particular, combinatorial optimization, distributed optimization, learning-based control, and their applications in multi-robot task allocation and motion planning in dynamic and adversarial environments.

Previously, I received an M.S. in Electrical & Computer Engineering from Georgia Tech in 2020 and a B.Eng. in Automation from Northeastern University (China) in 2018.
