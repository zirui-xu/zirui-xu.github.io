Ray Zirui Xu

I’m a Ph.D. candidate in Aerospace Engineering at University of Michigan, advised by Vasileios Tzoumas. My research interests lie in the intersection of optimization, control, and robotics. In particular, my work investigates: 1) provably near-optimal algorithms for online learning and combinatorial optimization; and 2) robot learning and coordination algorithms that enable effective and efficient online decision-making in dynamic and adversarial environments.

Previously, I received an M.S. in Electrical & Computer Engineering from Georgia Tech in 2020 and a B.Eng. in Automation from Northeastern University (China) in 2018.
