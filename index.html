Zirui Xu

I am an incoming Ph.D. student in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Michigan, advised by Vasileios Tzoumas. My research interests lie in algorithmic robotics, in particular, control theory, combinatorial optimization, reinforcement learning, and their applications in multi-robot task allocation and motion planning in dynamic and adversarial environments.

I received an M.S. in Electrical & Computer Engineering from Georgia Tech in 2020, advised by Kyriakos G. Vamvoudakis, and a B.Eng. in Automation from Northeastern University (China) in 2018.
